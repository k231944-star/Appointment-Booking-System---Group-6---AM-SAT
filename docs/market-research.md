# Market Research Report

## Competitor List

The following are common systems and solutions related to appointment booking:

1. Google Calendar  
2. Calendly  
3. Setmore  
4. SimplyBook.me  
5. Manual booking (phone calls / paper records)  

---

## Competitor Feature Comparison

### 1. Google Calendar

**Features offered:**
- create and manage events  
- calendar view  
- reminders and notifications  
- integration with email  

**Limitations:**
- not designed for booking services  
- no automatic booking system  
- requires manual scheduling  

---

### 2. Calendly

**Features offered:**
- online appointment scheduling  
- automatic time slot selection  
- email notifications  
- calendar integration  

**Limitations:**
- limited customization  
- some features require payment  
- not focused on full business management  

---

### 3. Setmore

**Features offered:**
- appointment booking system  
- customer management  
- calendar view  
- online booking page  

**Limitations:**
- limited flexibility  
- advanced features are paid  
- may be complex for simple use  

---

### 4. SimplyBook.me

**Features offered:**
- service booking system  
- schedule and staff management  
- notifications and reminders  
- customer data management  

**Limitations:**
- complex interface  
- pricing for advanced features  
- not suitable for small simple projects  

---

### 5. Manual Booking (Traditional Methods)

**Features offered:**
- simple and low cost  
- no technical setup  

**Limitations:**
- high risk of errors (double booking)  
- no real-time availability  
- difficult to manage data  

---

## Market Opportunity

There is a strong opportunity to develop a simple appointment booking system.

Many current systems are:
- too complex  
- require payment  
- not suitable for small businesses  

At the same time, many businesses still use manual methods, which are inefficient and can cause errors.

This creates a need for a system that is:
- simple  
- easy to use  
- affordable  
- focused on basic booking functions  

---

## Unique Value Proposition

The Appointment Booking System focuses on simplicity and usability.

It provides:
- easy booking process  
- clear time slot management  
- prevention of double booking  
- simple interface for users and admin  

Unlike complex systems, this project is designed for:
- small businesses  
- basic booking needs  
- easy understanding and use  

---

## Summary of Unsolved Problems

Even with existing systems, some problems still exist:

- systems are too complex or expensive  
- lack of simple and clear interface  
- manual booking causes errors  
- limited customization for small projects  

This shows the need for a simple and effective booking system.
