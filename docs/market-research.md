Competitor List

The following are some common systems and platforms related to appointment booking:

Google Calendar
A popular tool for scheduling and managing appointments.
Calendly
An online appointment scheduling system for meetings and bookings.
Setmore
A booking platform for small businesses such as salons and services.
SimplyBook.me
A booking system with features for managing appointments and customers.
Manual booking (phone calls / paper records)
Traditional method still used by many small businesses.
Competitor Feature Comparison
1. Google Calendar

Features offered:

create and manage events
calendar view
reminders and notifications
integration with email

Limitations:

not designed specifically for booking services
no built-in customer booking system
requires manual scheduling
2. Calendly

Features offered:

online appointment scheduling
automatic time slot selection
email notifications
integration with calendars

Limitations:

limited customization for small business needs
some features require payment
not focused on full business management
3. Setmore

Features offered:

appointment booking system
customer management
calendar view
online booking page

Limitations:

limited flexibility for customization
some advanced features are paid
may be complex for simple use cases
4. SimplyBook.me

Features offered:

booking system for services
service and staff management
notifications and reminders
customer data management

Limitations:

interface can be complex
pricing for advanced features
may be too heavy for small projects
5. Manual Booking (Traditional Methods)

Features offered:

simple and low cost
no technical setup required

Limitations:

high chance of errors (double booking)
no real-time availability
difficult to manage and track data
Market Opportunity

There is a strong opportunity to develop a simple and efficient appointment booking system.

Many existing systems are either:

too complex for small businesses
require payment for full features
not fully customized for specific needs

At the same time, many small businesses still use manual methods, which are inefficient and error-prone.

This creates a need for a system that is:

simple and easy to use
affordable
focused on essential booking features
Unique Value Proposition

The Appointment Booking System focuses on simplicity and usability.

It provides:

easy appointment booking for users
clear time slot management
prevention of double booking
simple interface for both users and admin

Unlike complex platforms, this system is designed for:

small businesses
simple booking needs
easy understanding and use
Summary of Unsolved Problems

Even with existing systems, some problems still exist:

systems are too complex or expensive
lack of simple and clear interface
manual booking still causes errors
limited customization for small projects

This shows the need for a simple and effective booking system.
